SftG starts partnership with Captain Planet Foundation

For 30 years, Captain Planet Foundation has been working collaboratively to engage and empower young people to be problem solvers for the planet. Since this is the very reason SftG exists, we have established a partnership so that we vitalize our impact and reach out to more youth.

Dialogue to Action: An honest and moving speech by our Signal

Marvin aired such a reflective and touching speech during the December Youth Conference. In a nutshell, Marvin challenged youth to push their limits. Below are his words;
“How do you explain when things don’t go as we assume? Or better, how do you explain when others are able to achieve things that seem to defy all of the assumptions?” We, the youth have a very powerful potential, we must have the courage to use this potential to change old ideas and practices towards the good ends.
